Partners and Accreditations


We take a lot of care over the suppliers we work with because we know our clients rely on us as their trusted and impartial technology advisor. We want to provide the best possible solutions to help them succeed through their ICT.


We’re quite fussy about the people we team up with. We do a lot of research to begin with, and when it comes to technology solutions we do a lot of testing too. We want to ensure that our partners will help us and our clients to succeed.

Once we’re satisfied, we work hard to build those partnerships, and commit to the highest accreditations and certifications and to undertaking in-depth training, thereby ensuring we have the necessary skills and knowledge to deliver their solutions and maximum value through our services.

We make sure that we have friends in all the right places, who are as committed to us as we are to them. The result is a community of people who’re dedicated to delivering benefits to each other – vendors, associations, ourselves, and our clients. As a result, we are all winners.
