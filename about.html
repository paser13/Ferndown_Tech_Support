About Us

Ferndown Tech Support

Just recently formed in January of 2017 by Ryan Parry and Elliot McMullen Managing directors and owners with one goal in mind to provide technical support whilst delivering fantastic customer service. Ferndown Tech Support are based in Ferndown and provide high quality IT support, training and advice to the residences and small business in the surrounding are.

As a IT Support company with a straightforward approach to delivering technical support with one single purpose to serve our clients with Incredible Support! We focus on you, our client to ensure you really get the value you deserve from our IT technicians. We are the “Go To” people who can offer solid advice that comes from putting ‘what you need’ before our own sales targets.

Our Focus is when something broke we fixed it making sure it stays fixed. But we wanted to offer customers something better than a break/fix service. We wanted to stop things breaking in the first place. So we offer Free advice and upgrade service alongside a pay as go training and Tutorials service.

We do not use ‘technical jargon’, instead we outline the best and most cost effective way of giving you support and advice, so that you will be in no doubt about exactly what you will be paying for. When you contact Ferndown Tech Support you will be dealing with friendly, personable and patient engineers who have many years of experience in dealing with every kind of hardware and software issue, covering a wide range of makes and models of Apple Macs, laptops and PCs.

Our ethos is one of honesty and professionalism with the sole aim to solve all your hardware and software troubles, at affordable fixed prices and to have happy and satisfied customers, who will return to use our services again in the future.
